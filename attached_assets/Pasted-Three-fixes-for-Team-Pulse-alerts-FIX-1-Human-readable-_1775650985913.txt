=== FIX 2: Double video on "як звільнитися" ===

When user sends "як звільнитися":
1. Pulse trigger detection fires → sends pulse_decision video
2. Preset q26 also fires → sends offboarding video

Result: user gets TWO videos — one from pulse, one from preset.

Fix: In the trigger processing code in telegram_webhook.py, 
when trigger_type is "звільнення" (resignation), do NOT send pulse video.
The preset system will handle the video response.

Find the line that calls send_pulse_video() for resignation triggers 
and skip it:

if trigger_type != "звільнення" and trigger_type != "resignation":
    await send_pulse_video(chat_id, video_id)

OR simply remove video sending for red (resignation) triggers entirely — 
the HR alert is the important part, the video comes from the preset.

=== FIX 3: Risk score capped at 10 ===

Current: "Рівень ризику: 15/10" — makes no sense.

In update_risk_score(), cap the score:
  current_score = LEAST(current_score + :pts, 10)

And in the alert message builder, cap display:
  risk_display = min(risk_score, 10)

Also in the DB update:
  SET current_score = LEAST(pulse_risk_scores.current_score + :pts, 10)
