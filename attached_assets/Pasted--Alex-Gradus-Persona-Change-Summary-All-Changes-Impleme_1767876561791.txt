**Professional Standard**:

**Answer to Your Question**: 
**Yes, AI can do this in real life!** LLMs can be explicitly trained to refuse answering when uncertain. The key is:
1. Clear instruction to admit uncertainty
2. Permission to say "I don't know"
3. Alternative actions (escalate, research, follow up)
4. Examples of when to refuse vs. when to answer

This prevents hallucination and builds user trust.
