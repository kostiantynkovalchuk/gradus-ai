Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 20, 2025

Introduction

Gradus Media ("we", "our", "us") operates the Maya AI assistant on Facebook Messenger. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our bot.

Information We Collect

When you interact with Maya on Messenger, we collect:

  • Messages: The text content of messages you send to Maya
  • User Information: Your Facebook name and Page-Scoped ID (PSID)
  • Conversation Context: Message history to provide relevant responses

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered responses about beverage industry marketing and brands
  • Improve our service and conversation quality
  • Understand user interests and common questions
  • Provide customer support when needed

AI Processing

Your messages are processed using:

  • Claude AI (Anthropic) for generating responses
  • OpenAI for semantic search and knowledge retrieval

These services process your messages to provide intelligent responses but do not store your data for training purposes.

Data Storage and Security

We store conversation data securely using:

  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Access controls and authentication

We retain conversation data for up to 90 days to improve service quality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Anthropic (Claude AI), OpenAI (embeddings) for bot functionality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your conversation data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your conversation history
  • Stop: Stop using the bot at any time by not sending messages

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data:

  1. Send a message to our page: "Delete my data"
  2. Or email us at: privacy@gradusmedia.com

We will delete your conversation history within 30 days of your request.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our Messenger bot or Facebook page.

Third-Party Links

Our bot may provide links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.
